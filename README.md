Provence Coffee Data Analysis Project
This project explores sales data from a regional coffee shop chain operating in three locations in Provence, France: Cassis, Éze, and Le Castellet. Utilizing a dataset of over 149,000 transactions, this analysis uncovers business performance, seasonal trends, and customer purchasing behavior.

🚀 The Hybrid Methodology (AI + Human Insight)
This project was executed using a hybrid approach, demonstrating a modern Data Analyst workflow:

AI-Assisted Technical Execution: Gemini (AI) was utilized to generate and optimize SQL/Python code for data cleaning and transformation, significantly reducing development time while ensuring robust syntax.

Human-in-the-Loop Analysis: The business logic, strategic hypothesis testing, data storytelling, and the final visualization design were manually curated to ensure that the insights are not just technically accurate, but also actionable and relevant to business stakeholders.

📊 Analysis Workflow
The analysis followed a structured path to provide a holistic view of the business:

Monthly Revenue Trends: Overall business performance analysis.

Branch Performance: Comparative analysis between Cassis, Éze, and Le Castellet.

Product Popularity: Ranking products by volume per location.

Seasonality: Monthly demand fluctuations for key products.

Operational Efficiency: Analysis of peak traffic hours (8:00 - 11:00).

Premium Strategy: The impact of high-end ($45) product offerings.

Customer Behavior: Average Order Value (AOV) by location.

Weekly Dynamics: Traffic distribution across the days of the week.

Final Dashboard: An integrated summary of business performance.

💡 Key Findings
Operational Peak: Our cafes experience a strong operational peak between 08:00 and 11:00, requiring optimized staffing during these hours.

Top Performing Branch: Le Castellet consistently achieves the highest Average Order Value (AOV), indicating a customer base with a higher propensity for premium purchases.

Premium Success: The $45 premium product line contributes disproportionately to total revenue despite lower volume, validating the premiumization strategy.

Growth: The chain demonstrates steady year-over-year revenue growth, with a strong finish in the peak summer months.
